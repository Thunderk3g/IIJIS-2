Information for Authors

A Complete Guideline

Indian Innovations: Journal of Indian Start-ups publishes articles, interviews, case studies and data analysis reports.

You can use this list to carry out a final check of your submission before you send it to the journal for review. Please check the relevant section in this Guide for Authors for more details.

Ensure that the following items are present:

  • Email address
  • Full postal address

For Manuscripts:

  • Files can be in the following formats: DOC, DOCX or PDF. Microsoft documents should not be locked or protected
  • Manuscripts must be submitted in English
  • Use a standard font and standard size
  • All figures, graphs, diagrams and tables must include captions and details
  • Indicate clearly if any colour is to be used for any figures in print
  • All references mentioned in the Reference list are cited in the text, and vice-versa
  • Permission has been obtained for use of copyrighted material from other sources (including internet)

Acceptance criteria:

All published works in Indian Innovations: Journal of Indian start-ups are peer-reviewed and must be:

  • scientifically sound
  • written in clear, concise and correct English
  • Original, unpublished work
