The Indian Innovations: Journal of Indian Start-ups is committed to publishing policy and business analysis related to start-ups. The journal encourages new ideas, creative approaches and analytical perspectives on areas such as: Entrepreneurship, Economics and Finance, Public Policy on start-ups and entrepreneurship, Leadership and Sustainable Development.

The Indian Innovations: Journal of Indian Start-ups is a student-run initiative that aims to delve into the start-up ecosystem in India. The journal aims to capture the success(and failure) stories of Indian Start-ups, combined with interviews from Industry Experts, case studies and data analysis and policy analysis to facilitate a comprehensive understanding of various elements that are necessary to create a strong Start-up ecosystem and spread awareness regarding the same. The journal emanates knowledge about the different aspects affecting the start-ups with a broader view. The journal is meant for researchers, students, and readers interested in the current issues and developments of the Indian start-ups. The journal will be published electronically. It will be easily accessible and free of charge, widely across the region. We also welcome views on recent business-relevant books, news, announcements, presentations of current research, and significant projects.
